Some Fun Facts

  1. The Chicago Bears were founded in 1920, at the time they were the Decatur Staleys.
  2. The Bears won a Super Bowl in 1985.
  3. The 1985 team is regarded as one of the best NFL teams of all time, with a famed defense known as the "Monsters of the Midway".
